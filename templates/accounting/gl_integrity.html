Accounting Governance Rules
Transaction → Journal Entry Mapping
  • AR Invoice: DR Accounts Receivable / CR Sales Revenue
  • Customer Payment: DR Cash / CR Accounts Receivable
  • AP Vendor Invoice: DR Inventory/Expense / CR Accounts Payable
  • AP Vendor Payment: DR Accounts Payable / CR Cash
  • Inventory Receipt: DR Inventory / CR Accounts Payable
  • WIP Issuance: DR WIP / CR Inventory
  • WO Completion: DR Finished Goods / CR WIP
  • COGS: DR Cost of Goods Sold / CR Inventory
GAAP Enforcement Rules
  • Every financial transaction requires a journal entry
  • Total debits must equal total credits (tolerance ≤ $0.01)
  • All lines must reference valid Chart of Accounts codes
  • Transactions and JEs must post atomically
  • Posted JEs are immutable — corrections via reversals only
Integrity Checks Performed
  • AR invoices (non-Draft/Void) without GL entry
  • AP vendor invoices without GL entry
  • Journal entries where debits ≠ credits
  • Journal entry headers with no lines
