General Ledger Posting Settings
When enabled, approving an invoice will automatically create GL entries for revenue recognition and accounts receivable. When disabled, you must manually post invoices to the GL after approval.
Important:
  • Automated posting will only occur for invoices approved after this setting is enabled.
  • Existing approved invoices will not be automatically posted.
  • GL posting requires the following accounts to exist in your Chart of Accounts:
    • 1120 - Accounts Receivable
    • 4100 - Sales Revenue
