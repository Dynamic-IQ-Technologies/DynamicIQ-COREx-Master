Understanding Net Requirements
Demand Sources
  • Sales Order Demand: Unfulfilled quantities from Confirmed, In Progress, or Pending sales orders
  • Work Order Demand: Materials required for Open, In Progress, or Released work orders (minus already issued)
Supply Sources
  • On Hand: Current inventory quantity
  • Pending PO: Quantities on order from Sent, Confirmed, or Approved purchase orders
Net Requirement = (Sales Demand + WO Demand) - (On Hand + Pending PO)
A positive net requirement indicates a shortage that needs to be addressed through purchasing or production.
