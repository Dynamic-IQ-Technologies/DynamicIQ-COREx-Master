Add a fully functional Shipping & Receiving Module to the application with the following capabilities and integrations:

1. Core Functionality

Manage and track all work order and sales order releases for shipping.

Allow creation, editing, and completion of shipping transactions.

Enable receiving transactions for incoming materials, components, or returns.

2. Shipping Management

Generate and manage shipment records linked to sales or work orders.

Allow assignment of tracking numbers, carriers, and shipping methods.

Support partial and full shipments, with validation against order quantities.

Automatically update inventory levels and order status upon shipment.

Enable printing of packing slips, shipping labels, and bills of lading.

3. Receiving Management

Manage receipts from purchase orders, returns, and inter-department transfers.

Validate received quantities against expected quantities.

Allow inspection or quality verification steps if required.

Automatically update inventory and system status after successful receiving.

4. Workflow Integration

Integrate seamlessly with:

Inventory Module – for stock adjustments.

Sales Module – for order fulfillment status.

Work Order Module – for manufacturing shipments and material receipts.

Accounting Module – for invoice and cost reconciliation.

5. Reporting & Visibility

Dashboard for current shipments, pending deliveries, and receipts.

Reporting tools for shipment history, on-time performance, and receiving discrepancies.

Audit trail for all shipping and receiving transactions.

6. User Experience

Clean, intuitive interface for warehouse and logistics users.

Mobile-friendly access for use on tablets or scanning devices.

Support barcode and label scanning for faster processing.

Goal:
Deploy a complete Shipping & Receiving Module that enables seamless management of all outbound (shipping) and inbound (receiving) transactions linked to sales and work orders, ensuring full visibility, control, and accuracy across operations.
