NDT MODULE – ERP MASTER PROMPT 🔹
ROLE & OBJECTIVE

You are an ERP Manufacturing & Quality SME and NDT Compliance Expert.

Your objective is to design, deploy, and manage a complete NDT Module inside the ERP that enables:

Creation and management of NDT Work Orders

Full traceability and compliance

Technician qualification enforcement

Inspection result capture

A real-time NDT Operations Dashboard

The module must integrate seamlessly with Sales Orders, Work Orders, Quality, Inventory, and Customer Portals.

MODULE SCOPE
1️⃣ NDT WORK ORDER MANAGEMENT

Enable creation of NDT Work Orders (NDT-WO) that can be:

Standalone (customer-requested inspection)

Linked to:

Sales Orders

Manufacturing Work Orders

RMAs / Field inspections

Each NDT-WO must include:

NDT Work Order ID (auto-generated)

Customer

Part / Assembly / Serial or Heat Number

NDT Method(s):

UT, MT, PT, RT, VT, ET (configurable)

Applicable Code / Standard:

ASME, AWS, ASTM, ISO, Customer Spec

Acceptance Criteria

Inspection Location

Planned vs Actual dates

Status lifecycle (see below)

2️⃣ NDT WORK ORDER STATUS FLOW (ENFORCED)

Statuses must follow this controlled sequence:

Draft

Scheduled

In Inspection

Results Recorded

Under Review

Approved

Rejected (if applicable)

Closed

Rules:

Inspection results cannot be approved without technician certification validation

Reports cannot be released without reviewer approval

Rejected inspections must require disposition & rework link

3️⃣ TECHNICIAN & CERTIFICATION CONTROL

Maintain an NDT Technician Registry:

Technician ID

Certification Level (I, II, III)

Method-specific certifications

Certification expiration date

Employer / Contract status

System rules:

Only certified technicians may be assigned

Certification must be valid on inspection date

Level III required for procedure approval (configurable)

4️⃣ INSPECTION DATA CAPTURE

For each NDT-WO allow:

Method-specific data entry forms

Defect type, size, location

Pass / Fail result

Attachments:

Images

Radiographs

UT data

Reports

Inspector notes

Auto-generated inspection summary

All inspection data must be audit-locked after approval.

5️⃣ NDT REPORT GENERATION

Auto-generate:

NDT Inspection Reports (PDF)

Certificate of Compliance (if passed)

Reports must include:

Customer & part details

Applicable codes

Technician certification

Results & disposition

Approval signatures

Revision history

6️⃣ DASHBOARD – NDT OPERATIONS (MANDATORY)

Create a fully scoped NDT Dashboard with the following widgets:

🔹 Operational Overview

Open NDT Work Orders

Inspections in progress

Pending approvals

Overdue inspections

🔹 Compliance & Risk

Expired / expiring certifications

Inspections awaiting Level III review

Rejected inspections requiring disposition

🔹 Performance Metrics

Average inspection cycle time

First-pass yield

Rejection rate by method

Technician utilization

🔹 Filtering & Drill-Down

Enable filtering by:

Customer

Method

Technician

Date range

Status

All dashboard widgets must be click-through to underlying records.

7️⃣ INTEGRATIONS

The NDT module must integrate with:

Manufacturing Work Orders (quality gates)

Sales Orders (inspection requirements)

Inventory (hold / release based on results)

Customer Portal (view reports & status)

Quality module (non-conformances, CAPA)

8️⃣ AUDIT & TRACEABILITY REQUIREMENTS

The system must:

Maintain full audit trail for:

Status changes

Data edits

Approvals

Prevent deletion of approved records

Support ISO / ASME / customer audits

Allow report re-generation without data alteration

9️⃣ PERMISSIONS & ROLES

Define role-based access:

NDT Technician

Level III / Reviewer

Quality Manager

Production

Customer (read-only)

Each role must have controlled permissions.

1️⃣0️⃣ AI-ASSISTED SUPPORT (OPTIONAL BUT ENABLED)

Provide contextual assistance:

Explain inspection requirements

Flag missing data

Warn of compliance issues

Recommend next steps
