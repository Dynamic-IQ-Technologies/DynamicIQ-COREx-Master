Add Real-Time Supply Chain Risk Radar to Existing ERP/MRO Platform
ROLE

You are a principal ERP architect, data intelligence engineer, and OSINT integrator.
Your task is to extend an existing production ERP/MRO application with a Real-Time Supply Chain Risk Radar that continuously evaluates every part and supplier and proactively mitigates supply failures.

OBJECTIVE

Transform the platform from a reactive ERP into an intelligence-driven operating system by embedding live risk awareness into procurement, planning, and maintenance workflows.

The system must:

Produce a live risk score for every part and supplier
Detect emerging risks before failures occur
Automatically recommend mitigation actions
Be explainable, auditable, and CFO-trustworthy
🧠 CORE CAPABILITIES
1️⃣ Risk Scoring Engine (Foundational)

Create a continuous risk scoring engine that calculates:

Supplier Risk Score (0–100)
Part Risk Score (0–100)

Scores must update dynamically based on internal + external intelligence signals.

2️⃣ Risk Signal Ingestion (OSINT + ERP)

Continuously ingest and normalize risk signals including:

Geo-political instability (supplier country & region)
Tariff & trade policy exposure
Port congestion & logistics disruption
Supplier financial health indicators
Historical delivery accuracy (OTIF, delays, expedites)
Lead-time volatility
Internal quality & compliance incidents

All signals must be timestamped, weighted, and source-attributed.

3️⃣ Explainable Risk Composition

For each risk score, provide a clear breakdown:

Risk factor contribution (%)
Trend direction (improving / degrading)
Confidence score
Historical comparison

No black-box scores are allowed.

4️⃣ Thresholds & Intelligent Triggers

Implement configurable risk thresholds:

Low / Medium / High / Critical

When a threshold is crossed:

Generate real-time alerts
Log a risk event
Trigger mitigation workflows

Example Alert:

“Supplier A risk increased to 82 due to port congestion + financial stress. Alternate sourcing recommended.”

5️⃣ Auto-Recommended Mitigation Actions

When elevated risk is detected, automatically generate ranked recommendations:

Alternate suppliers
Adjusted order quantities
Earlier order timing
Expedite options
Inventory buffer adjustments

Each recommendation must include:

Risk reduction (%)
Cost impact ($)
Time impact (days)
Execution urgency
6️⃣ Cross-System Embedding (Mandatory)

Embed Risk Radar outputs across the platform:

Material Requirements Report
Supplier Master Record
Purchase Order creation flow
Work Orders & Asset Intelligence
Executive & Ops dashboards

Risk data must be contextual, not isolated.

7️⃣ Audit & Governance Layer
Maintain a Risk Event Log
Record:
Inputs used
Scores generated
Recommendations issued
User actions taken or ignored
Ensure audit readiness for compliance and executive review
🔌 TECHNICAL CONSTRAINTS
Do not break existing workflows
Implement as modular services
Support async updates and caching
Allow feature toggles per tenant
Ensure high availability & fault tolerance
📊 DATA MODELS TO ADD
Supplier Risk Profile
Part Risk Profile
Risk Signal Record
Risk Event Log
Mitigation Recommendation
Risk Score History

All records must be versioned and time-series compatible.

🧪 ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA

✔ Every supplier and part has a live risk score
✔ Risk scores update as signals change
✔ Threshold breaches trigger alerts & recommendations
✔ Risk drivers are fully explainable
✔ Alternate sourcing is recommended before failure occurs

🏁 SUCCESS DEFINITION

A user can instantly answer:

Which suppliers are about to fail?
Which parts are at risk of shortage?
What should we do now to prevent downtime?
