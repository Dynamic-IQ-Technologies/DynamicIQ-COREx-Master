Create an Executive Accounting Dashboard within the app to provide leadership with real-time visibility into the organization’s financial performance — including revenue, expenses, payables, receivables, and profitability trends — in a single, interactive view.

Functional Requirements:

Dashboard Access & Layout:

Add a new top-level dashboard page titled “Executive Accounting Dashboard.”

Ensure it is accessible only to users with executive or accounting management roles.

Use a responsive, card-based layout with high-level KPIs at the top and detailed charts below.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
Display the following summary metrics as real-time cards:

Total Revenue (MTD / YTD)

Total Expenses (MTD / YTD)

Gross Profit Margin (%)

Total Accounts Payable (Open / Due)

Total Accounts Receivable (Open / Overdue)

Cash on Hand / Available Balance

Outstanding Vendor Payments

Outstanding Customer Invoices

Net Income to Date

Detailed Visuals & Charts:
Include the following interactive visualizations:

Revenue vs. Expense Trend (Monthly Line Chart)

Accounts Receivable Aging (Bar Chart)

Accounts Payable Aging (Bar Chart)

Top 10 Customers by Revenue (Horizontal Bar or Pie Chart)

Top 10 Vendors by Spend (Horizontal Bar or Pie Chart)

Cash Flow Forecast (Line or Area Chart)

Profitability by Department or Program (Stacked Column or Donut Chart)

Data Sources:

Pull data directly from the General Ledger, Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, and Bank Reconciliation modules.

Ensure the dashboard refreshes automatically at least once per hour or on demand.

Aggregate data for both monthly and year-to-date views.

Filtering & Drill-Down:

Allow executives to filter the dashboard by:

Date Range

Business Unit / Department

Vendor or Customer

Program / Project Code

Enable drill-down functionality — e.g., clicking on a KPI or chart opens the supporting transactions or GL details.

Design & User Experience:

Use color-coded visual indicators (green for positive trends, red for declines, yellow for warnings).

Apply consistent financial formatting for currency and percentages.

Include tooltips or hover details for each chart and KPI.

Present a clean, executive-level interface with minimal navigation clutter.

Security & Permissions:

Restrict visibility to authorized executive users and finance staff only.

All financial data must comply with internal security and data privacy standards.

Additional Features:

Option to export the dashboard as a PDF or Excel summary report.

Include a “Last Updated” timestamp to show when data was last refreshed.

Integrate a “Quick Links” section for shortcuts to A/R, A/P, and GL reports.

Expected Outcome:

The Executive Accounting Dashboard provides leadership with an immediate, accurate, and actionable overview of the company’s financial health.

Enables faster decision-making through visual analytics and real-time performance indicators.

Reduces the need for manual financial reporting by automating aggregation and presentation of accounting data.
