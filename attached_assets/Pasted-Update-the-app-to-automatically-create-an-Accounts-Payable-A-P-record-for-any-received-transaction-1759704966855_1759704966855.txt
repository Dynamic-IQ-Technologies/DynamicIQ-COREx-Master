Update the app to automatically create an Accounts Payable (A/P) record for any received transaction (e.g., Purchase Order Receipt, Repair Order Receipt, or Service Receipt) that requires supplier or vendor payment.

Functional Requirements:

Trigger Point:

Automatically generate an A/P record when:

A Purchase Order or Repair Order is marked as Received or Partially Received.

The receipt includes billable material, labor, or service costs.

Exclude customer-supplied or non-billable items.

Accounts Payable Record Structure:

Create a new A/P record with the following key fields populated automatically:

Vendor Name / Supplier (from the original PO or RO)

Vendor Invoice Number (blank or system-generated placeholder until invoice entry)

Purchase Order / Repair Order Reference

Receipt Number (auto-linked to the transaction)

Receipt Date

Amount Due (based on received quantities × unit price)

Tax, Freight, or Additional Charges (if applicable)

Payment Due Date (based on vendor terms)

GL Account Codes (linked automatically to material or expense accounts)

Status: “Pending Invoice” or “Awaiting Payment”

Automatic Numbering:

Generate a unique A/P Transaction ID for each payable created (e.g., “AP-0001234”).

UI Integration:

Add an Accounts Payable section or sub-tab under the related Purchase Order / Repair Order / Receipt.

Include fields for quick visibility of:

Amount Due

Due Date

Payment Status

Linked Receipt # and PO #

Accounting Integration:

Automatically post journal entries upon A/P creation:

Debit: Inventory or Expense Account

Credit: Accounts Payable Liability Account

Ensure all GL entries are tracked with transaction references for traceability.

If invoice reconciliation occurs later, allow update of the A/P amount but log all changes in an Audit Trail.

Validation & Controls:

Validate that the supplier has valid payment terms and is active.

Prevent duplicate A/P creation for the same receipt.

Display a warning or block creation if the vendor invoice already exists for the PO/receipt.

Workflow & Notifications:

Send a system notification or email to the Accounting Department or A/P Clerk when a new payable is generated.

Optionally, queue A/P entries for approval before posting to the ledger.

Reporting & Dashboards:

Add a dashboard or report view to monitor:

Open Payables

Payables Aging Summary (by vendor, due date, or amount)

Payment Status and Reconciliation Progress

Expected Outcome:

Upon material or service receipt, an A/P record is automatically created and linked to the supplier and original transaction.

The system captures all required financial details, ensuring accounting accuracy and timely vendor payments.

The A/P record is fully integrated with the ledger, audit trail, and reporting modules.
