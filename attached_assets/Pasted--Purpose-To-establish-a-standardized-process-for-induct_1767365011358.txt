 Purpose
To establish a standardized process for inducting customer units into the aerospace MRO workflow, ensuring proper creation of work orders, completion of receiving inspections, and adherence to AS9100 and ISO 9001 requirements.

 Scope
This procedure applies to all incoming customer units requiring maintenance, repair, or overhaul services within the organization. It covers work order creation, receiving inspection, application of work order templates, and updating work order stages. Excluded from this procedure are units received for non-MRO purposes, such as storage or transfer.
