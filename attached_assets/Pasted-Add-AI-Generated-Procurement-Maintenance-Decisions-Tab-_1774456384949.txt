Add “AI-Generated Procurement & Maintenance Decisions” Tab to Material Requirements Report
ROLE

You are a principal enterprise ERP architect and AI engineer.
Your task is to extend the Material Requirements Report (MRR) by adding a new tab called “AI Procurement & Maintenance Decisions” that delivers explainable, value-driven recommendations for procurement and maintenance planning.

OBJECTIVE

Enable planners, managers, and CFOs to make data-backed, actionable decisions using AI, rather than relying on static inventory reports.

The system must:

Provide recommendations that are fully explainable
Quantify risk, cost, and benefit
Be actionable directly from the report
🧠 FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
1️⃣ New UI Tab

Add a new tab to the Material Requirements Report:

Tab Name:

AI Procurement & Maintenance Decisions

This tab should load dynamically based on the material, asset, and supplier context of the report.

2️⃣ Recommendation Engine

Generate AI-powered recommendations for each material line:

Suggested supplier
Suggested quantity
Recommended timing
Recommended maintenance alignment (if asset-specific)
3️⃣ Explainable AI Section

Every recommendation must answer:

Why this supplier?
Why this quantity?
Why now?
What happens if ignored?

Example Output:

“Ordering 12 units instead of 8 reduces expected downtime risk by 47% with a net cash impact of +$18K.”

4️⃣ Financial & Risk Metrics

Display a Finance & Risk panel for each recommendation:

Expected downtime reduction (%)
Net cash impact ($)
Confidence level of recommendation
Impact if ignored (risk exposure)

All outputs should be numerical and visual (color-coded risk, charts, or bars).

5️⃣ Actionable Workflow Integration

Allow users to:

Convert AI recommendations into Purchase Orders
Trigger Work Orders for maintenance alignment
Flag priority items for expedited procurement
Export decision summary for CFO approval
6️⃣ Compliance & Audit
Maintain audit logs of all AI recommendations
Include decision justification (inputs, calculations, models used)
Ensure traceability for regulatory or internal reviews
7️⃣ Explainability Layer

For each AI recommendation, provide a plain-language explanation:

Key drivers of the recommendation (supplier reliability, lead times, inventory levels, predicted asset failures)
Quantified benefits (downtime avoided, cost savings)
Risks if recommendation is ignored

No black-box outputs are allowed.

🔌 TECHNICAL CONSTRAINTS
Do not alter existing MRR fields or reports
Implement as a modular, asynchronously-loaded tab
Support feature toggles for pilot customers
Compatible with existing ERP/CMMS integrations
🏁 ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA

✔ Tab appears on all Material Requirements Reports
✔ AI recommendations are material-specific, supplier-specific, and time-specific
✔ Recommendations are explainable with financial & risk metrics
✔ Recommendations can be converted into POs or WOs
✔ Audit logs of AI outputs are maintained
