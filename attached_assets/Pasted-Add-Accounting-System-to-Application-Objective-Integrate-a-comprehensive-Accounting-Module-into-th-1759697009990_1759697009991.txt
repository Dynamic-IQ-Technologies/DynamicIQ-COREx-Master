Add Accounting System to Application

Objective:
Integrate a comprehensive Accounting Module into the current application to ensure proper financial management, reporting, and compliance with accounting standards. The system should automate entries from operational activities, maintain audit trails, and support real-time reporting.

1. Core Accounting Structure
Chart of Accounts (COA)

Configurable COA with account types:

Assets: Cash, Accounts Receivable, Inventory, Fixed Assets

Liabilities: Accounts Payable, Accrued Expenses, Loans

Equity: Retained Earnings, Owner’s Capital

Revenue: Sales, Services, Miscellaneous Income

Expenses: COGS, Labor, Overhead, Administrative Expenses

Support hierarchical grouping for reporting.

Allow adding, editing, or deactivating accounts with role-based permissions.

2. General Ledger (GL)

Maintain double-entry accounting for all transactions.

Features:

Automatic posting from operational modules (Purchasing, Sales, Work Orders)

Manual journal entry creation for adjustments

Posting/unposting control

Audit trail with user, date/time, and transaction source

3. Subledgers & Module Integration
Accounts Payable (AP)

Post vendor invoices automatically from POs.

Track outstanding payables, due dates, and partial payments.

Automatic postings:

Dr. Inventory / Expense

Cr. Accounts Payable

Accounts Receivable (AR)

Post customer invoices automatically from sales or work orders.

Track payments, partial payments, and aging.

Automatic postings:

Dr. Accounts Receivable

Cr. Revenue / Tax Liability

Inventory Accounting

Track inventory valuation (FIFO, LIFO, Average Cost).

Post material receipts, issues, and work order consumption.

Ensure inventory valuation integrates with GL.

Work Order Accounting

Track labor, material, and overhead costs per work order.

Post WIP during work execution, and COGS upon completion or sale.

4. Journal Entries

Support manual and recurring journal entries.

Ensure debit = credit validation.

Approval workflow for high-value or adjusting entries.

Attach supporting documents for audit purposes.

5. Financial Reporting

Provide real-time reports and dashboards:

Standard Reports

Trial Balance

Balance Sheet

Income Statement / P&L

Cash Flow Statement

AP & AR Aging

Inventory Valuation

GL Detail

Dashboards

Revenue vs Expenses

Cash Position

Top Vendors & Customers

Work Order Cost Summary

6. Internal Controls

Role-based access (Admin, Accountant, Viewer).

Segregation of duties for approvals.

Lock periods after financial close.

Maintain immutable audit logs.

7. Compliance

Comply with GAAP (or IFRS if applicable).

Support fiscal calendar configuration.

Standardized numbering for invoices, receipts, and journal entries.

Maintain full audit-ready transaction history.

8. Integration with Operational Modules

Purchases → AP

Sales → AR

Inventory → GL and WIP/COGS

Work Orders → Labor, Material, and Overhead costs

9. Expected Outcome

After implementation, the application will:

Automate financial postings from operational activities.

Maintain accurate, audit-ready financial records.

Enable comprehensive financial reporting and analysis.

Ensure compliance with proper accounting practices.
