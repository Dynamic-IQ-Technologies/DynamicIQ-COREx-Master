"Create a fully functional Sales Module in my app that can manage all types of sales transactions, including outright sales, exchanges, and managed repairs. The module should include the following functionality:

Sales Types & Transactions

Support for outright sales, exchanges, and managed repair sales.

Ability to select the sales type when creating a sales order.

Automatically track inventory impact based on sales type.

Customer Management

Link each sales order to a customer account.

Store customer billing and shipping addresses.

Maintain a history of all sales transactions per customer.

Inventory Integration

Deduct sold items from inventory automatically.

For exchanges and managed repairs, track cores, replacements, and returned items.

Include serialized product support if applicable.

Order Details

Include line items with part numbers, descriptions, quantities, prices, discounts, and tax.

Allow attachments and comments per line item.

Capture expected repair details for managed repair sales, including return dates and service notes.

Pricing & Payments

Support multiple pricing types, including customer-specific pricing, core charges, and repair charges.

Track partial payments, deposits, and final invoicing.

Automatically generate invoices upon order completion.

Workflow & Status Tracking

Track sales order status (e.g., Draft, Pending, Shipped, Completed, Returned).

For managed repairs, track repair progress, return to stock, or return to vendor status.

Reporting & Dashboards

Provide dashboards for sales performance, outstanding orders, inventory impact, and revenue by sales type.

Include export options for accounting and operational review.

Validation & Error Handling

Ensure required fields are completed before saving a sales order.

Prevent overselling inventory.

Validate data integrity for managed repair and exchange transactions.

The goal is to create a comprehensive Sales module that integrates seamlessly with inventory, customer management, and accounting, supporting all standard MRO sales operations."
